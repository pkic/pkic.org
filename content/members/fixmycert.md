## Interactive PKI Education for Practitioners

FixMyCert is a vendor-neutral PKI education platform built for the people who actually manage certificates — DevOps engineers, SREs, security professionals, and PKI administrators. The platform provides over 200 free, ungated resources including interactive demos, practical guides, compliance trackers, troubleshooting tools, and governance templates that translate complex PKI standards, browser root policies, and regulatory requirements into clear, actionable guidance.

Born from years of hands-on enterprise PKI experience at major financial institutions, FixMyCert addresses a persistent gap in the PKI ecosystem: practitioners need practical implementation guidance, not marketing materials or academic theory. The platform takes a "disgronification" approach — converting dense CA/Browser Forum ballots, root program policies, and compliance frameworks into step-through visual experiences that teams can use during real certificate operations and incident response.

The FixMyCert PKI Compliance Hub tracks CA/Browser Forum ballot timelines, certificate validity changes, and regulatory deadlines across standards including PCI DSS, DORA, NIS2, and NIST frameworks, helping organizations prepare for changes before they become emergencies. All content remains completely free and ungated — no email walls, no paywalls — reflecting a commitment to advancing PKI literacy across the industry.

As a PKI Consortium member, FixMyCert is committed to making consortium standards and best practices accessible to the broader practitioner community.
