# QTrino Labs

QTrino Labs combines applied cryptography research with enterprise security execution to help organizations transition to quantum-safe operations with measurable risk reduction and audit-ready governance.

**Built for regulated environment and long-lived confidentiality requirements.**

## Our Conviction
**Post-quantum readiness is not an algorithm swap. It is a governance and sequencing challenge.**

Regulated enterprises face a three-layer cryptographic governance problem — and most are dealing with all three at once.

First, cryptographic assets are scattered across systems without a reliable system of record. Second, risks such as certificate expiry, weak algorithms, and configuration drift are detected during audits rather than proactively. Third, findings remain technical, with no clear translation into business impact — making prioritisation, budget allocation, and executive sponsorship difficult.

Post-quantum readiness compounds this challenge. Without a complete inventory, there is no assessment baseline. Without a baseline, there is no credible migration roadmap.

QTrino was founded to solve this problem in that sequence — inventory, assessment, and then migration.
