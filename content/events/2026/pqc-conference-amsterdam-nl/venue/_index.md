## Venue

The conference takes place at **[Meervaart](https://meervaart.nl/en/meetings-events/)**, a modern event venue located between the Amsterdam city center and Schiphol Airport.

**Address:** Meer en Vaart 300, 1068 LE Amsterdam, The Netherlands

## Getting there

### Public transport

Amsterdam public transport (GVB) serves Meervaart directly. Use [9292.nl](https://9292.nl/en/?naar=amsterdam_meer-en-vaart-300) to plan your journey.

- **Tram 1** — stop *Meer en Vaart*
- **Tram 17** — stop *Osdorpplein Oost*
- **Bus 369** — stop *Osdorpplein Oost*

### By car

Use [Google Maps](https://www.google.nl/maps/dir/de+Meervaart+Theater,+Meer+en+Vaart+300,+1068+LE+Amsterdam/@52.358988,4.807397,17z) for directions to Meer en Vaart 300, Amsterdam.

### Parking

**Q-Park Osdorpplein** — 1-minute walk from the venue  
Osdorpplein 249, 1068 SW Amsterdam  
The first hour is free; €2.00 per 30 minutes thereafter.

**Parking Garage Nieuw-West** — 5-minute walk from the venue (subject to availability)  
Jan Celestraat 11, Amsterdam (entrance under Jumbo Osdorpplein)  
Conference attendees can use this garage at a reduced day rate of €12.50.

A designated disabled parking space is available directly in front of the main entrance (not reservable; free with a disabled parking card).
