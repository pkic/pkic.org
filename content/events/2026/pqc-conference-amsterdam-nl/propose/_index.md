## Session proposal

Propose a session led by practitioners or subject-matter experts for the Post-Quantum Cryptography Conference Amsterdam 2026. We welcome a wide range of formats, including lightning talks, fireside chats, panel discussions, presentations, deep dives, and interactive workshops that share real migration experience and practical takeaways. You can add co-presenters or panelists during submission, and after submitting you will receive a manage link to update your proposal and keep session details current.

### What we are looking for

- **No commercial or promotional talks.** Product demos, hidden marketing messages, and self-promotional statements are not allowed.
- **Experience-led content.** Prioritize real implementation experience over theory-only content.
- **Clear lessons and takeaways.** Tell attendees what they can apply immediately after your session.
- **High audience interaction where relevant.** Formats such as facilitated discussions, role-play exercises, and breakout groups are encouraged.
